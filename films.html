Our Films

She Moves Africa Films produces bold, emotional, and socially conscious stories by and about African women. Because art is a powerful tool for change, a unique way to bring people together.

With Grace

A 30-minute short documentary following Grace, a witty 13-year-old girl in rural Kenya. Through her story, we see the real effects of climate change on families and girls at the frontlines of climate injustice. Told with warmth and humor, the film is a catalyst for action on gender and climate justice.

Watch the official trailer for "With Grace"

Film Highlights

Premiered at IDFA (Netherlands)
Winner of the Children's Jury Award – One World Festival
Broadcast on BBC iPlayer (UK)
Selected by festivals in Australia, Greece, Slovakia & Norway

Film for Impact

Amplifies Girls' Voices

The film shows the strength, dreams, and lived experiences of girls facing the climate crisis head-on, creating visibility and agency.

Mobilizes Community Awareness

Through culturally relevant, community-led screenings, we use storytelling to start REAL conversations about climate injustice and its impact on children.

Encourages Action

We bring people together to talk, learn, and act—especially in the places most affected by climate change—while activating direct resources to girls and families.
