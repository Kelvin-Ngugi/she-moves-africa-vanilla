Our Story is Your Story

She Moves was born from a one woman's whisper and now turned into a community's embrace. At our core is a belief that women are strongest when they rise for each other.

Our Why: Healing, Joy, Power

In every corner of Africa, there are women carrying dreams bigger than their circumstances, girls with visions that could reshape continents, and communities waiting for the spark that ignites transformation.

We exist because we believe in the power of African women. We believe in healing that happens in circles, joy that multiplies when shared, and power that emerges when sisters stand together.

Our why is simple: because every African woman deserves to see herself reflected in stories of strength, to feel held by a community that understands her journey, and to know that her voice can move mountains.

Our Journey: From Whisper to Movement

2020

The Whisper

A single voice speaking truth about African women's experiences, starting conversations that would spark a movement.

2022

The Circle

First community gatherings form, creating safe spaces for women to share stories, heal together, and build sisterhood.

2024

The Movement

Hundreds of women across multiple countries unite, creating lasting change through films, wellness, and community action.

Meet Our Founder: Dina Muthama

An award-winning Producer and Showrunner with over 20 years of experience creating impact-driven content across East Africa. Known for bold storytelling that blends entertainment with social change, her work includes Makutano Junction, Shamba Chef, The Know Zone, and Faithless.

Dina is also a passionate mentor, shaping the next generation of African filmmakers through hands-on apprenticeship and advocacy.

"When women come together in truth and vulnerability, magic happens. We heal not just ourselves, but our communities, our daughters, and generations to come." - Dina Muthama

Our Films

She Moves Africa Films produces bold, emotional, and socially conscious stories by and about African women. Because art is a powerful tool for change, a unique way to bring people together.

With Grace

A 30-minute short documentary following Grace, a witty 13-year-old girl in rural Kenya. Through her story, we see the real effects of climate change on families and girls at the frontlines of climate injustice. Told with warmth and humor, the film is a catalyst for action on gender and climate justice.

Watch the official trailer for "With Grace"

Film for Impact

Amplifies Girls' Voices

The film shows the strength, dreams, and lived experiences of girls facing the climate crisis head-on, creating visibility and agency.

Mobilizes Community Awareness

Through culturally relevant, community-led screenings, we use storytelling to start REAL conversations about climate injustice and its impact on children.

Encourages Action

We bring people together to talk, learn, and act—especially in the places most affected by climate change—while activating direct resources to girls and families.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get involved with She Moves Africa?

There are many ways to get involved! You can join our wellness programs, participate in our community events, volunteer as a mentor, or partner with us on projects. Contact us to learn more about current opportunities.

Do you have programs outside of Kenya?

Currently, our physical hubs are located in Kenya, but we welcome women from across Africa to join our online community and participate in virtual events and programs.

How can organizations partner with you?

We welcome partnerships with organizations that align with our mission. Contact us at collaborations@shemovesafrica.org to discuss potential partnership opportunities.
